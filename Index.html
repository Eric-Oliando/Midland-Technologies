YOUR TECH JOURNEY



Midland Technologies

We are an innovative organization established to empower youths aged 16 and 24 by bridging the gap between high school education and global opportunities in technology. We aim to equip participants with essential technical and soft skills, foster an entrepreneurial mindset, provide access to a network of global opportunities, create sustainable livelihoods, and bring economic change to society.

Mission: To train thousands of youths and young people in Kakuma with quality and accessible technical and soft skills that set them apart to compete for global market opportunities.

Wether you are a beginner, or a seasoned professional looking to enhance your dynamic career and develop, these skills will be indispensable for boosting your career prospects and making a positive impact in your workplace, our institution is your home.

There is significant illiteracy and a need for more tech skills, hindering talent acquisition, economic growth, and innovation in Kakuma.

Our effort is the scope of opportunities for Kakuma and its youths in the digital market.

Our Learners

Vision: A future where young persons in Kakuma have unlimited access to tech opportunities to contribute meaningfully to the global tech ecosystem by learning, earning, and solving problems encountered, leading to sustainable, social, and economic development.

Our core values

Resilient, persistent, and adaptability
